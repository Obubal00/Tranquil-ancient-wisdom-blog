It was during my final year at university, surrounded by textbooks and deadlines, that I discovered the Tao Te Ching. While everyone around me was pushing harder, sleeping less, and burning out faster, I stumbled upon a different way:

This ancient wisdom contradicted everything I'd been taught about productivity. Instead of forcing, it spoke of allowing. Instead of striving, it suggested flowing. And it worked.
